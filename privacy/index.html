Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2025

Bulpara Inc ("we", "our", or "us") operates the OutfitMaker mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Photos you upload: When you use OutfitMaker, you upload photos of yourself to generate outfit visualizations. These photos are sent to our servers for processing and are stored temporarily to deliver results.

Generated images: The AI-generated outfit images are stored on our servers so you can access them from your history.

Device information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics and debugging purposes.

Purchase information: If you subscribe to Premium, payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but never see your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI outfit visualizations from your photos
  • To maintain your generation history within the app
  • To process and verify Premium subscriptions
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements (free tier only)

Photo Storage and Deletion

Your uploaded photos and generated images are stored on secure cloud servers (Cloudflare R2). You can delete your generation history at any time from the Settings screen. When you delete your data, both the original photos and generated images are removed from our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Replicate: AI model hosting for outfit generation. Your photos are sent to Replicate's servers for processing. See Replicate's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising for free tier users. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Billing: Subscription management. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission and secure cloud storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

OutfitMaker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your data from within the app (Settings > Clear All Data)
  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Contact us to request deletion of your data from our servers

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
